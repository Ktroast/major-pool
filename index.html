The Covenant
House rules

    Entry

  • $20 per entry.
  • Each entrant selects five golfers, one from each tier, based on the Official World Golf Rankings.
  • Tier 1: OWGR 1–10 · Tier 2: 11–20 · Tier 3: 21–30 · Tier 4: 31–40 · Tier 5: 41 and beyond.

    • Scoring

  • Each entrant's total uses the best 4 of 5 golfers (worst score dropped).
  • Lowest total score wins. Displayed throughout as score relative to par.

    • Missed Cut & Withdrawals

  • Any golfer who misses the cut or withdraws after play begins receives a score equal to: the highest 4-round score of any player who made the cut, plus 10 strokes.

    • Tiebreaker

  • Each entrant submits a predicted winning score.
  • Closest prediction to the actual winning score wins the tiebreaker.

    • Payout

  • 1st Place: $200.
  • 2nd Place: $40.

    • General

  • The Commissioner retains every right to change the rules at any moment. All decisions are final.
